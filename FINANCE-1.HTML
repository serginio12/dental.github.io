FINANCE

We are delighted to be able to offer our patients 0% finance on their treatment costs.

FINANCE

Are you considering considerable treatment, Orthodontic or Cosmetic work, but not sure about the best way to pay for it? If so, you might be interested in the interest free payment plans we offer to fund your treatment.

How much can I borrow?

You can borrow between £600 and £50,000, subject to status. You must be over 21 to apply.

How do I apply?

You will need to complete a simple, online credit application, which one of our staff can help you with in the surgery. Most applications are approved straight away, so that you can go ahead immediately to book an appointment for the treatment you want.

How much will I pay?

With our 0% facility, you pay the costs of your treatment - no more and no less. Interest free means just that! There are no hidden costs and no deposit required!

Insurance companies will process your claim and send payment or pay directly into your bank account.

The amount of money paid depends on what level of insurance you have. Before you start treatment, it is useful to discuss your cover with your dentist to confirm the amount of remuneration.

Routine check-ups and dental hygiene appointments are usually covered in full.

For other dental treatments (fillings, crowns, inlays, root canals, etc) a percentage of the total amount is paid (ranging from 50% to 100%) again depending on your cover.

Emergency one-off treatment is usually covered 100% (seeing a patient on the day with extreme toothache to get that patient out of pain).

If you have any questions, please ask any member of our team and we will gladly explain how you could benefit from it.
